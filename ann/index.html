Second anniversary — Dec 10, 2024

Taiga Torako / 大合虎子

Torako used to live in Japan's tiny Tiger Shrine, the guardian deity of tigers and cats. 500 years old. Torako was born on June 15, and is a lover of hot springs, tea (yuru, or sencha), and sashimi, but is intolerant of and has a hard time with noise and mechanical operations.
